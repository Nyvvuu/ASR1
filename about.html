About

Based on the principles of sisterhood, cultural diversity, and Asian-interest, the founding members of Alpha Sigma Rho came together at the University of Georgia in 1998. They founded Alpha Sigma Rho to provide unity and support within the campus and surrounding community. These women had a shared desire to cultivate an organization that promotes strong leadership, academic excellence, moral integrity, and service. Through their vision, Alpha Sigma Rho has grown and thrived at many institutions.

Since its founding, Alpha Sigma Rho has established chapters across Texas, Pennsylvania, Virginia, and South Carolina. While the core values of Alpha Sigma Rho remain the same, the sorority has expanded its commitment to multicultural awareness and academic growth. We welcome women from all walks of life and value diversity, individuality, and the strength that unity brings.
